space-pv-materials▼
Python, JARVIS, LightGBM, XGBoost, Random Forest, DFT plus eDMFT
$ python train_pv_model.py --materials jarvis
Trained models like LightGBM and XGBoost on around ten thousand materials from the JARVIS database to classify strong photovoltaic candidates. Ran DFT plus eDMFT and phonon calculations to study threshold energy and vibrational stability. Used OMERE and SR NIEL to model radiation damage and end of life performance, and shipped a shortlist of durable PV materials for space missions.
